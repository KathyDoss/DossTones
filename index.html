We are a group of professionals, including a couple of attorneys, a music teacher, a licensed clinical social worker, an interior landscape designer..., and we have all been playing music in and around Nashville most of our lives!!

We play a lot of soul, blues, jazz, some pop oldies, some country and a bit of bluegrass. The sax, B3/piano, guitar, and banjo give us range, and there is a lot of singing.

We're flexible, dependable, and we enjoy playing early shows. In addition to clubs and parties we have played benefits for the homeless. We are available for good causes. We are doing this because we love it. Give us a try..., we're confident you'll like what you hear!

In the BIOs link there is a short bio and a video excerpt featuring each player. The Band Videos link is to a 24 song show from Springwater, courtesy of videographer Arnie Reed.
