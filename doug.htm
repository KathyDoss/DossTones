Doug Aitken (piano, organ, guitar, vocals) is a licensed clinical social worker and an exceptional multi-instrumentalist. He worked in the Nashville music business for over 25 years and helped clients Keith Urban, Tim McGraw, The Judds, and more develop their careers. Doug is also an accomplished guitarist and showcases those talents in his band, The Motones. Doug is the father of Trevor Aitken, the artist who designed The DossTones logo.
